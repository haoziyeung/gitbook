#### 统计vcf文件中突变类型：
```
zcat in.vcf.gz | vcftools_0.1.9/bin/vcf-annotate --fill-type | grep -oP "TYPE=\w+" | sort | uniq -c

bcftools stats file.vcf > file.stats
```
